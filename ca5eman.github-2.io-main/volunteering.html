Bread of Life

The Bread of life is a local food pantry located in Oceanside California. During my senior year of highschool I volunteered with them every couple of mornings for about four hours. The team I worked with there are the most down to earth people I have met, one of them even gave me an extra snowboard he had lying around.

KidsGames

From 2015 to 2018 I was a small group leader at my local church, North Coast Calvary Chapel, for their summer KidsGames camp. I was assigned to help the kidergarten section of the camp which proved to be a unique challenge. I kept the children safe and grouped together. Made sure they behaved properly both to each other and the instructors at the camp. I will never forget how many extra snacks they let us leaders keep, which definitely kept me sane.
